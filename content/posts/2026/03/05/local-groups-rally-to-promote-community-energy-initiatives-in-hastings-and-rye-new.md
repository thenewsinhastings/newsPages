In a significant show of community solidarity, various local groups have rallied their support behind the call to 'unlock community energy at scale' within Hastings and Rye. The initiative aims to empower local communities by providing them with more control over their energy needs through collective action.

Representatives from several environmental organizations met recently at the Town Hall to discuss how they can collaborate with residents, businesses, and council officials to enhance renewable energy projects in both towns. These efforts are not only expected to reduce carbon footprints but also to foster a sense of community engagement and ownership over sustainable initiatives.

The groups emphasize that by working together, they can address the challenges faced in implementing large-scale green energy solutions and ensure that the benefits reach all members of society. They hope to inspire other communities across the region to follow suit, setting a precedent for future collaborative efforts towards environmental sustainability.
