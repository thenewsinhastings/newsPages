The local police are appealing to the public for any information that could help them in their investigation into a tragic fatal collision that occurred on Tuesday evening in St Leonards. The incident took place at approximately 7:30 PM near the intersection of Marine Parade and Belle Tropicana, where a pedestrian was struck by a vehicle.

Officers are particularly interested in speaking with anyone who may have witnessed the collision or any suspicious activity in the surrounding area leading up to the event. Anyone with information that could aid the investigation is urged to contact the police at their local station or through their website's online reporting system.

The family of the deceased has been informed, and a statement will be released from them once they are ready to speak publicly about this heartbreaking loss.
