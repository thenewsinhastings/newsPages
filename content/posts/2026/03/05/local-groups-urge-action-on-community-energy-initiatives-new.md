A recent initiative by local groups in Hastings and Rye is encouraging the community to unlock energy at a larger scale, focusing on renewable sources and sustainability. The campaign advocates for more substantial local involvement in energy production and distribution, aiming to reduce carbon footprints and promote environmental responsibility.

Organizers of the event highlighted the importance of collaboration between residents, businesses, and government bodies to create an effective community-led energy system. They argue that such a move can lead to economic benefits as well as positive impacts on local biodiversity and air quality.
