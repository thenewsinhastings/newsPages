A coalition of local groups in Hastings and Rye is advocating for the expansion of community energy initiatives to help reduce carbon emissions and promote sustainability. The campaign, titled 'Unlock Community Energy at Scale,' aims to encourage local residents and businesses to invest in renewable energy projects.

Supporters argue that by harnessing the power of collective action, communities can play a significant role in addressing environmental challenges while also creating economic opportunities through green technology. The initiative includes plans for solar panel installations on public buildings, wind turbine projects, and community-owned renewable energy cooperatives.

Organizers have called on local authorities to provide support and incentives for such ventures, emphasizing the importance of collaboration between government bodies, private sector companies, and community members in achieving meaningful progress towards environmental goals. They hope that by scaling up these efforts, Hastings and Rye can set an example for other towns and cities across the region.
