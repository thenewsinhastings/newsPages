Local fans of sci-fi comedy **Red Dwarf** are in for a treat as it has been announced that actor Chris Barrie, who played the iconic character Kryten, will be making an appearance at this year’s Hastings Comic Con.

Scheduled to take place on March 17th, 2026, the event is set to attract hundreds of visitors from across the region. Attendees can look forward to interacting with Chris Barrie during autograph signings and photo opportunities.

Organizers of Hastings Comic Con are thrilled at the prospect of bringing a star from such a beloved series to their annual event, which continues to grow in popularity year after year. It promises to be an exciting day for all comic book enthusiasts, sci-fi fans, and anyone who enjoys celebrating pop culture.
