In a significant push for sustainable energy solutions within Hastings and Rye, local groups have united in support of the campaign to 'unlock community energy at scale'. This initiative aims to empower communities by enabling them to generate their own renewable power, reducing reliance on traditional grid sources and fostering greater environmental responsibility.

Supporters argue that such a move would not only reduce carbon emissions but also provide economic benefits to local residents through job creation in the green technology sector. Various organisations, including local businesses and community centres, have expressed their backing for this vision, seeing it as a vital step towards achieving net-zero targets set by both regional authorities and national government.

Campaign organisers are inviting all interested parties, from environmental advocates to business owners, to join forces and advocate for policy changes that would facilitate the implementation of these energy projects. Public meetings will be held across Hastings and Rye in the coming weeks, offering an opportunity for residents to learn more about how they can contribute to this exciting initiative.
