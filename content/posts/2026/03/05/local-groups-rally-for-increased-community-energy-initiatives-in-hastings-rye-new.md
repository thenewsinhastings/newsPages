In a significant development, local groups and community activists have launched an initiative aimed at 'unlocking community energy' across Hastings and Rye. This ambitious project seeks to enhance the local capacity for renewable energy production by empowering communities with resources and support needed to establish their own sustainable energy solutions.

The initiative highlights the growing need for community-led approaches in tackling climate change and reducing carbon emissions within the region. With a focus on practical applications, the campaign advocates for easier access to funding, streamlined regulatory processes, and greater public awareness about renewable technologies such as solar panels and wind turbines.

Supporters hope that these efforts will not only lead to environmental benefits but also provide economic opportunities by creating jobs in the green sector and reducing energy costs for local residents. The project is set to engage with various stakeholders including local councils, businesses, and educational institutions to ensure a collaborative approach towards achieving its goals.
