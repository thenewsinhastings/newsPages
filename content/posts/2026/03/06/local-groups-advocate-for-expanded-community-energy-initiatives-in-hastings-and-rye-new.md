In a recent meeting at the Town Hall, local groups from Hastings and Rye have called for increased community involvement in energy projects. The aim is to 'unlock community energy at scale', thereby promoting sustainability within the region.

Speakers at the event highlighted the importance of collective action towards reducing carbon footprints and fostering a greener future for both towns. They emphasized that local communities play a crucial role in driving forward renewable energy solutions and ensuring they are accessible and affordable to everyone.

The initiative aims not only to reduce environmental impact but also to boost economic growth by creating jobs within the green sector, thereby benefiting residents of Hastings and Rye directly.
