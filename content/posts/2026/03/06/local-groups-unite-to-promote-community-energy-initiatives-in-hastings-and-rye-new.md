Residents and local groups from Hastings and Rye have come together to advocate for the expansion of community energy projects. The initiative, aimed at increasing renewable energy usage in the area, seeks to unlock a greater scale of sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and the economy.

The campaign highlights the importance of communal involvement in reducing carbon footprints and fostering a greener future. Local leaders are expected to discuss the proposals with stakeholders and government representatives in upcoming meetings.
