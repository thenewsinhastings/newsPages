In a significant push towards sustainable living within the Hastings and Rye area, several local groups have joined forces to advocate for increased community energy projects. The initiative aims to 'unlock community energy at scale,' promoting renewable energy solutions that can benefit both residents and businesses in the region.

The collaborative effort involves various organizations dedicated to environmental conservation, green energy solutions, and sustainable development. These groups believe that by working together, they can accelerate the transition towards cleaner, more accessible energy sources for the local population.

Supporters of this initiative argue that community-led projects not only reduce carbon footprints but also foster a sense of unity among residents. Local businesses are encouraged to participate in these initiatives as part of their corporate social responsibility efforts.

For more information about upcoming events and how to get involved, interested individuals can contact the local environmental group 'Green Energy for Hastings.' The organization will be hosting an open meeting next week where community members can learn about proposed projects and express their opinions on potential energy solutions for the area.
