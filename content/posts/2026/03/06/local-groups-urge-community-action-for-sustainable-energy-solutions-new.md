In a recent meeting at the Hastings Town Hall, local environmental groups have come together to call on residents and businesses in Hastings and Rye to embrace community energy projects that can be scaled up for greater impact. The initiative aims to unlock significant potential within communities by encouraging collective action towards renewable energy solutions.

Representatives from various organizations highlighted the importance of collaborative efforts in addressing climate change challenges faced by the region. They emphasized that through pooling resources and expertise, smaller groups could achieve larger-scale benefits in terms of sustainability and community resilience.

Attendees also discussed potential projects such as solar panel installations on public buildings, wind turbine cooperatives, and energy efficiency programs for local homes and businesses. The hope is to inspire more individuals and organizations to join the movement towards a greener future.

For those interested in participating or learning more about upcoming events related to community energy initiatives, contact details of key groups involved will be made available soon through various local platforms.
