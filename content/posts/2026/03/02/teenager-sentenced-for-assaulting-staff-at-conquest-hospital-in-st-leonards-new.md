A teenager has been jailed after pleading guilty to assaulting staff members at the Conquest Hospital in St Leonards, a report from More Radio reveals. The incident took place within the hospital's premises and caused significant concern among healthcare workers and visitors alike.

The court proceedings have not disclosed specific details about the nature of the assault or the identity of those involved due to legal restrictions. However, it is understood that such incidents can create an unsafe environment for both patients and staff members who are dedicated to providing essential medical care.

Local authorities emphasized the importance of maintaining a secure and safe healthcare setting for all visitors. The hospital management has also expressed its commitment to ensuring that appropriate measures will be taken to prevent future occurrences and support those affected by such incidents.
