In a recent development, several local groups in Hastings and Rye have come together to advocate for the expansion of community energy projects. The initiative aims to unlock greater potential within the region by promoting renewable energy sources and fostering community involvement.

The coalition includes representatives from environmental organizations, local businesses, and community leaders who believe that collective action can lead to significant progress towards sustainable living. They are calling on the Hastings Borough Council to support these efforts with policy changes and funding opportunities.

“By working together, we can create a greener future for our communities,” said Sarah Green, spokesperson for one of the participating organizations. “We need to harness local resources and knowledge to make real change happen.”

The groups are planning several public events throughout the year to raise awareness about community energy and encourage more residents to get involved.
