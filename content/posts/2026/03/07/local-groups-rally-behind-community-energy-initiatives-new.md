A coalition of local groups in Hastings and Rye have united to advocate for the expansion of community energy projects. The initiative, aiming to ‘unlock community energy at scale,’ seeks to empower residents through renewable energy solutions that are locally owned and managed.

The campaign emphasizes the importance of sustainable development and highlights how such initiatives can strengthen the local economy while reducing carbon emissions. Supporters argue that by investing in solar panels and wind turbines, communities like Hastings and Rye can not only meet their own energy needs but also contribute positively to national renewable targets.

Organizers hope to engage with local residents, businesses, and councils to develop practical steps towards implementing these projects. They believe this approach will foster a sense of community ownership and responsibility for the environment, as well as provide economic benefits such as job creation and cost savings on energy bills.
