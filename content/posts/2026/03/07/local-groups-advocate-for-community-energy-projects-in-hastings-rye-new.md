Residents and community groups in Hastings and Rye have joined a national initiative aimed at unlocking the potential of 'community energy' on a large scale. The movement seeks to empower local communities to take control of their own renewable energy projects, thereby reducing reliance on fossil fuels and promoting sustainability.

The call has been echoed by several local organisations which advocate for better access to clean energy solutions that can benefit both individuals and businesses within the area. This initiative is expected to not only contribute towards environmental goals but also create economic opportunities through job creation and reduced energy costs.

Community members interested in joining or supporting these efforts are encouraged to contact their nearest community group or attend upcoming public meetings scheduled for the next few weeks.
