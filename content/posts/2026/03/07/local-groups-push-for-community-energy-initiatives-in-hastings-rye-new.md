In an effort to drive forward sustainable energy solutions within the local community of Hastings and Rye, several groups have come together to support a campaign aimed at 'unlocking community energy at scale.' This initiative seeks to empower local residents by providing them with greater control over their energy production and consumption.

The coalition includes representatives from various sectors including environmental conservationists, local businesses, and educational institutions. They argue that fostering such initiatives can not only reduce carbon footprints but also stimulate economic growth in the area through job creation and investment opportunities.

As part of this effort, workshops are being planned to educate both residents and stakeholders about renewable energy options available locally, as well as how they can contribute towards more sustainable practices within their communities. The groups hope that these collaborative efforts will set a precedent for future projects aimed at environmental sustainability in Hastings & Rye.
