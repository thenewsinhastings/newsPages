The historic Warrior Square Station Bowl in Hastings faces potential changes as new local plan proposals are set to be reviewed by the council. Residents and heritage enthusiasts have voiced concerns over possible alterations that could impact this significant area.

Warrior Square, located near the heart of Hastings, is an important site both historically and culturally. The station bowl, which includes the old railway land, has been a focal point for local community activities and serves as a reminder of the town's rich industrial past.

The proposed changes come at a critical time when the area is seeking to balance heritage preservation with modern development needs. Council officials have assured that public consultations will be held to gather feedback from residents and stakeholders before any final decisions are made.

As discussions continue, community groups are encouraged to participate actively in the planning process to ensure their voices are heard regarding the future of Warrior Square Station Bowl.
