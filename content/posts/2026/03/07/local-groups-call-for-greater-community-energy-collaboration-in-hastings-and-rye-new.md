In a concerted effort to enhance local sustainability initiatives, various community groups in Hastings and Rye have joined together to advocate for the 'Unlocking Community Energy at Scale' campaign. The initiative aims to foster collaboration between different organisations to promote renewable energy projects that benefit both communities.

The campaign seeks to address rising energy costs and climate change concerns by encouraging residents and businesses to adopt more sustainable practices. Local leaders from Hastings Borough Council and Rye Town Council have shown support for the initiative, highlighting its potential impact on reducing carbon footprints while also creating jobs within the region.

Residents are being encouraged to participate in community meetings scheduled throughout May to discuss ideas and strategies that can be implemented locally. These gatherings will serve as a platform for residents to share their views and contribute to shaping future policies related to energy usage and conservation in Hastings and Rye.
