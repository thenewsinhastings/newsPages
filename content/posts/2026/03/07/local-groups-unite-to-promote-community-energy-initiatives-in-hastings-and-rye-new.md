Several local groups in Hastings and Rye have come together to support the 'unlocking of community energy at scale' campaign, aiming to increase renewable energy production within their communities.

The initiative encourages residents and businesses to explore ways to generate clean energy on a larger scale, potentially through solar panels, wind turbines, or other sustainable technologies. The goal is not only to reduce carbon footprints but also to create economic opportunities for local people.

Representatives from various community groups emphasized the importance of collaboration between individuals, organizations, and governmental bodies in achieving these ambitious goals. They highlighted several successful small-scale projects as a testament to what can be accomplished when communities work together towards common objectives.

For more information about how you can get involved or learn more about these initiatives, visit [communityenergyhastings.co.uk](http://communityenergyhastings.co.uk) or contact the local environmental committee directly.
