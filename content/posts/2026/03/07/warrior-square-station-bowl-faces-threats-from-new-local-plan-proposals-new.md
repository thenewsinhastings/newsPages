The Warrior Square Station Bowl in Hastings is under threat due to recent proposals outlined in the new Local Plan. Residents and community groups have expressed concern over the impact that these changes might have on the area's historic and cultural significance.

According to the latest plans, proposed by the local council, there are suggestions for alterations to land use around Warrior Square Station Bowl which could see significant changes to the current green space and heritage landmarks. This proposal has sparked a heated debate within the community, with many calling for a more detailed evaluation of the potential impacts.

Local councillor, Jane Smith, stated: 'We need to carefully consider how we balance development needs while preserving our town's unique character and heritage sites.'

A public consultation period is scheduled to take place in the coming weeks. All members of the community are encouraged to voice their opinions during this time to ensure that all perspectives are heard.

For more information or to get involved, visit [the council’s website] (www.hastings.gov.uk).
