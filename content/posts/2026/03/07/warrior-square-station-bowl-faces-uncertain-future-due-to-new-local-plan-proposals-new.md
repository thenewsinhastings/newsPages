The future of the beloved Warrior Square Station Bowl in Hastings is under threat due to new proposals in the local planning document. The plan, which aims to reshape the urban landscape and promote sustainable development, has sparked controversy among residents concerned about the impact on this historic landmark.

Local councillors are reviewing the proposals that could see changes to the area around the bowl, a popular spot for both locals and visitors. Conservationists and community groups have expressed concerns over potential alterations that might affect the bowl's historical significance and its role as a communal space.

The debate surrounding these plans highlights the ongoing tension between modern development needs and preserving local heritage sites. The council is set to hold public consultations in the coming weeks, inviting feedback from residents before making any final decisions.

As discussions continue, stakeholders are encouraged to engage actively with their elected representatives to ensure that the voice of the community is heard during this crucial period.
