Residents and community groups in Hastings are rallying to protect the Warrior Square Station Bowl as new Local Plan proposals pose a threat to its future. The iconic bowl, located in the heart of Hastings, has been a focal point for both locals and tourists alike, offering recreational space and historical significance.

The proposed changes have sparked concern among stakeholders who fear that development plans could compromise the unique character and functionality of this beloved public area. As consultations continue, activists are calling on councillors to take into account the widespread support for preserving the bowl’s current use and layout.

Council officials are urging members of the community to participate in upcoming discussions about the proposed amendments. The public is encouraged to provide feedback on how best to balance development needs with the preservation of valued communal spaces like Warrior Square Station Bowl.
