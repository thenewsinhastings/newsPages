The future of the beloved Warrior Square Station Bowl in Hastings is under threat as new proposals from the local council are set to impact its status and use. The plan, which aims to reshape parts of Hastings town centre, has sparked concerns among residents who cherish this green space for its recreational value and historical significance.

Local campaigners have expressed worry that the inclusion of the Station Bowl in redevelopment plans could jeopardize its preservation as a public area. They argue that it serves not only as an important landmark but also contributes significantly to the quality of life for local residents, providing a tranquil oasis amid urban development.

As discussions around the new Local Plan proposals progress, community members are encouraged to engage with their elected representatives and attend upcoming council meetings to voice their opinions on this critical issue. The fate of the Warrior Square Station Bowl remains uncertain as Hastings continues to navigate its path towards future growth and regeneration.
