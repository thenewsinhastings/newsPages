A woman from St Leonards has been charged with serious crimes involving drug trafficking and human trafficking. The charges follow an extensive investigation by local law enforcement agencies which uncovered a network of criminal activity affecting the community.

The arrest is part of ongoing efforts to combat illegal activities that threaten public safety and well-being in Hastings and Rye districts. Local authorities emphasize their commitment to protecting residents from such threats, encouraging anyone with information about similar crimes to come forward.

Details regarding court proceedings and further legal actions will be released by the relevant agencies as they become available.
