A woman from St Leonards has been charged with multiple serious crimes including drug trafficking and human trafficking, local police have confirmed. The charges come after an extensive investigation led by the police in collaboration with national law enforcement agencies.

Details of the case remain under strict confidentiality to protect ongoing legal proceedings. However, it is understood that the suspect was arrested following a coordinated operation aimed at dismantling an organised crime network operating across the region.

The local community has been advised to be vigilant and report any suspicious activity or information related to drug trafficking or human exploitation to law enforcement authorities immediately.
