A woman from St Leonards-on-Sea has been charged with serious crimes involving drug trafficking and human trafficking, local police have confirmed.

The incident occurred after a joint operation between the local police force and other law enforcement agencies. The suspect is alleged to be involved in an extensive network operating across multiple counties.

Police have stated that this arrest is part of ongoing efforts to combat these serious crimes within the region. They are urging anyone with information on similar activities or knowledge about missing persons cases related to trafficking to come forward and assist their investigation.

Residents concerned about safety and security in their community are encouraged to stay informed through official police communications and report any suspicious activity directly to local authorities.
