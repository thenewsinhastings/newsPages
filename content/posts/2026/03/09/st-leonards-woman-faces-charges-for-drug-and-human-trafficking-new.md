A woman from St Leonards has been charged with serious crimes including drug trafficking and human trafficking, the police have confirmed. The charges come after a prolonged investigation involving local law enforcement agencies.

The alleged offences took place in various locations around Hastings over an extended period. The accused is said to have engaged in activities that endangered public safety and violated numerous laws designed to protect individuals from exploitation and harm.

Local community members are being advised by police to remain vigilant and report any suspicious activity immediately. Residents are encouraged to contact the appropriate authorities if they suspect human trafficking or drug-related crimes within their area.

Further details of the case will be released in due course, pending legal proceedings.
