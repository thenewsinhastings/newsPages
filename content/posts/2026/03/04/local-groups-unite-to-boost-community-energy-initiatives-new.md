Several local groups in Hastings and Rye have joined forces to advocate for the expansion of community energy initiatives. The coalition, made up of environmental activists, business leaders, and citizens, is calling on the council to support the 'unlocking' of energy projects at a larger scale.

The movement aims to enhance renewable energy solutions within the region, promoting sustainability and reducing carbon footprints. By working together, these groups hope to increase public awareness about the benefits of community-driven energy projects and encourage more local residents to participate in such initiatives.

As part of their efforts, the coalition plans to organize workshops and information sessions across both towns over the coming months. These events will provide residents with detailed insights into how they can contribute towards a greener future for Hastings and Rye.
