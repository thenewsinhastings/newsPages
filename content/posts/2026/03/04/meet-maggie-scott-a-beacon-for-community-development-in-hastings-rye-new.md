Maggie Scott is a name that resonates deeply within the community of Hastings and Rye. Known for her tireless dedication to improving local services and fostering a sense of unity among residents, Maggie's impact extends beyond mere recognition. Over the years, she has spearheaded numerous initiatives aimed at enhancing the quality of life in both towns.

With a career spanning over two decades, Maggie has demonstrated unwavering commitment to community development. Her latest project focuses on revitalizing the local youth centre, aiming to provide young people with safe spaces and opportunities for growth. This initiative not only addresses immediate needs but also sets a foundation for future generations.

In addition to her work in youth services, Maggie is actively involved in various charity organisations within Hastings and Rye. Her efforts have been instrumental in raising awareness about local issues such as mental health support and environmental sustainability.

For those who know Maggie well, her passion and energy are infectious. She continually inspires others to contribute positively to their community, embodying the spirit of collaboration that is essential for fostering a thriving environment for all residents.
