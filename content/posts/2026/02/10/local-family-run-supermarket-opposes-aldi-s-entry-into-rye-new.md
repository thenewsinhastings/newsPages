Jempson's, the only large supermarket in Rye, has come out against plans for a new Aldi store in the town. The family-run business has expressed concerns over the potential impact on its local operations and the community's long-standing support for locally owned enterprises. This decision highlights ongoing debates about the effects of national chains entering smaller towns versus supporting established local businesses.
