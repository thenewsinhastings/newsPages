Local residents are invited to join the upcoming meeting of the United Nations Association (UNA) branch in Hastings. The discussion, scheduled for next week, will focus on pressing issues undermining world peace and ways to address them. This event is a platform for community members interested in international affairs and global cooperation.

The UNA aims to promote understanding and support for the work of the United Nations among the public and policymakers. Members and guests can expect an engaging debate covering topics such as conflict resolution, human rights, environmental sustainability, and other critical challenges facing humanity today.
