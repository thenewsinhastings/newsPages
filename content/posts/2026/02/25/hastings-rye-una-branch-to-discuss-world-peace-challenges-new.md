The Hastings and Rye branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to hold a significant debate on issues threatening world peace. The meeting, scheduled for next week at the town hall, will see local residents and community leaders engage in discussions about pressing global concerns such as climate change, geopolitical tensions, and social inequalities.

As part of their mission to promote international cooperation and understanding, the UNA branch aims to raise awareness among the public on these critical issues. The event promises to be an insightful exchange of ideas and perspectives, offering a platform for local voices in addressing global challenges.

For more information or to attend the debate, interested members of the community are encouraged to contact the Hastings & Rye UNA branch directly.
