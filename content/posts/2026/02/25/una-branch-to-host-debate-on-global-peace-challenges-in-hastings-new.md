The United Nations Association (UNA) branch in Hastings is set to host a debate focusing on the critical issues undermining global peace. The event, which promises to be an enlightening and engaging discussion, will take place at the local community centre next week.

Speakers from various backgrounds including international relations experts, local activists, and members of the public will present their views and engage in discussions about pressing challenges such as conflict resolution, human rights abuses, and environmental degradation. The debate aims to raise awareness among residents and encourage active participation in initiatives that promote peace globally and locally.

Residents are invited to attend and contribute to the discussion by sharing their thoughts on how communities can play a role in fostering international peace and understanding. For more information or to register for the event, visit the UNA Hastings branch website.
