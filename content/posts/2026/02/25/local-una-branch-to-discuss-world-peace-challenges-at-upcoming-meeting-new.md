The Hastings and Rye United Nations Association (UNA) branch is set to host a thought-provoking debate on the pressing issues currently undermining global peace. Scheduled for next month, the event will provide a platform for members and guests to delve into critical topics affecting international stability.

This meeting aims to foster dialogue around current challenges such as geopolitical tensions, environmental crises, and socio-economic disparities that threaten world peace. Attendees are encouraged to engage in constructive discussions and share their insights on how local communities can contribute towards global efforts for a more peaceful planet.

For those interested in attending or participating remotely, further details will be provided closer to the event date.
