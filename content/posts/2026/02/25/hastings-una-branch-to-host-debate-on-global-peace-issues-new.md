The Hastings branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) will hold a debate this Thursday evening at the Town Hall, focusing on the key issues that are undermining world peace. The event aims to bring together community members and local politicians to discuss pressing global challenges such as conflict in Ukraine, political instability in the Middle East, and environmental crises.

Attendees will have the opportunity to voice their opinions and engage with experts who will provide insight into how individuals can contribute to efforts towards a more peaceful world. The debate is open to everyone interested in making a difference and understanding the complexities of global politics and peace initiatives.

For further details or to reserve your place, contact the Hastings UNA branch via email at hastingsuna@unassociation.org.
