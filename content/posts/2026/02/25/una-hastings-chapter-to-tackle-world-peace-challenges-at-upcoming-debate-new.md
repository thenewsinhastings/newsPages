The United Nations Association (UNA) Hastings chapter is set to convene for a crucial debate on the pressing issues that undermine world peace. The event, scheduled to take place at the Grand Hall in Hastings town centre, will feature prominent speakers and experts discussing global challenges such as climate change, political instability, and human rights violations.

Attendees from across the region are expected to participate in the discussion, aiming to raise awareness about the importance of international cooperation and peacebuilding efforts. The debate is part of a series of events organized by UNA branches nationwide, highlighting the significance of local communities in addressing global issues.

For those interested in attending or for further information on how to get involved, please contact the Hastings branch of UNA through their official website.
