The United Nations Association (UNA) branch in Hastings is set to hold a debate on pressing issues that are undermining world peace. This local event will provide an opportunity for community members and experts to engage in discussions about global challenges such as conflict, inequality, and climate change.

Attendees will have the chance to contribute their insights and ideas towards finding solutions to promote international stability and cooperation. The meeting is open to all interested individuals and aims to foster a greater understanding of global peacekeeping efforts among Hastings residents.

For more information about the event or to become involved with UNA's work in Hastings, please contact the branch directly.
