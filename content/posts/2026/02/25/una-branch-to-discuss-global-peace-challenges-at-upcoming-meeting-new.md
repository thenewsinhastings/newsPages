The Hastings and Rye branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to hold a crucial meeting this week, focusing on the pressing issues that are currently undermining global peace. The event aims to bring together local residents, political figures, and community leaders for an open discussion about the challenges facing international stability.

The debate will cover topics such as conflict resolution, human rights violations, economic instability, and environmental crises. Attendees are encouraged to participate actively in order to contribute their thoughts on how local communities can play a role in global peace efforts.

For those interested in attending or supporting the initiative, more information is available from the Hastings and Rye UNA branch office.
