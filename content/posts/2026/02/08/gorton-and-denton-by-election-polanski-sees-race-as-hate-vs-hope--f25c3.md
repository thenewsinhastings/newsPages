Leader Zack Polanski of the Reform Party has issued a stark call to voters in Gorton and Denton, declaring that this upcoming by-election will be a critical moment for determining whether community hope or division will prevail. As he gears up for the contest against the Green Party, Polanski's message aims to galvanise support among residents who are looking towards the future with optimism.

The Reform Party leader has been actively campaigning in both areas, engaging directly with voters on issues such as environmental sustainability and economic development. He believes that his party’s vision for progress offers a clear alternative to what he perceives as negativity from other political factions. With polls suggesting a tight race between the Greens and Reform, Polanski's rhetoric underscores the significance of this local election in shaping broader regional politics.

Local voters are being encouraged to consider their stance on key issues like renewable energy projects and community investment, which have become central themes in Polanski’s campaign. As Hastings & Rye residents weigh their options, they will play a crucial role in determining the direction of these influential local elections.
