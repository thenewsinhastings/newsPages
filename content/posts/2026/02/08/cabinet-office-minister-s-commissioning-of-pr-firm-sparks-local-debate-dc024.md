In a recent development with local political implications, Cabinet Office Minister Josh Simons commissioned the services of APCO Worldwide in 2023 to investigate journalists' sources. This move has raised questions among residents and local politicians about transparency and privacy concerns within government operations.

The decision by Minister Simons to engage a private PR firm for such an investigation has drawn attention across Hastings & Rye, with many community members expressing interest in understanding the implications of this action on local governance and media relations. As debates continue, the community awaits further clarifications from the Cabinet Office regarding the scope and outcomes of this engagement.

Local politicians have expressed a range of views, highlighting the importance of open dialogue and accountability in ensuring that public interests are served effectively. This story underscores the ongoing conversation about government transparency and its impact on local democracy.
