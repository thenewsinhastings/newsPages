The annual Heritage Festival, which kicked off last week, features guided tours of historic buildings, live music performances by local artists, and interactive workshops for all ages.

One of the highlights is an exhibition at the Rye Art Gallery showcasing artefacts from Rye's storied past, including ancient manuscripts and medieval coins.
