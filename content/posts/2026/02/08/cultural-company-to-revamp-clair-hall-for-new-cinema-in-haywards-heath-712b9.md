A local cultural company is set to transform Clair Hall in Haywards Heath, Hastings & Rye region, into a modern four-screen cinema. The development aims to bring new cultural opportunities and entertainment options for residents and visitors alike. The project is expected to enhance the community’s vibrant arts scene and provide additional facilities that will benefit both locals and tourists.

As part of their initiative, the unnamed company has taken on the redevelopment of Clair Hall, which has been a significant landmark in the area. This new cultural venue promises to attract more visitors to Haywards Heath and support local businesses as well.

Further details about the project’s timeline and additional features will be released shortly by the developers.
