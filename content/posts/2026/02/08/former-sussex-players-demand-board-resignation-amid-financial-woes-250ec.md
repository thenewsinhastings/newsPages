In a move that has sent shockwaves through the local cricket community, several high-profile former Sussex players have called for the club's board of directors to resign. The players’ demand comes amid ongoing financial difficulties at the club, which has left many fans and supporters concerned about the future of their beloved team.

The call for resignation highlights the growing frustration among those closely associated with the club over recent management decisions and transparency issues surrounding the club’s finances. While Sussex Cricket is based outside of Hastings & Rye, its impact resonates throughout our region due to the club's storied history and numerous ties within the community.

Local cricket enthusiasts and club members are encouraged to voice their opinions through official channels as discussions around the club’s future continue.
