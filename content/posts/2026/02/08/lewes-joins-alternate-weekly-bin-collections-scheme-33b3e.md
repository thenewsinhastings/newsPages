Residents of Lewes are set to experience a new approach to waste management as the town adopts an alternate weekly bin collection scheme. The initiative, designed to promote recycling and manage non-recyclable waste more effectively, will see recyclables collected in one week while non-recyclable waste is picked up the following week.

This change comes as part of broader efforts by local authorities to enhance environmental sustainability within the Hastings & Rye region. Lewes joins a growing list of towns across East Sussex implementing similar schemes aimed at reducing landfill waste and encouraging community participation in recycling initiatives.
