The coastal town of Hastings continues to witness a surge in community-driven efforts aimed at preserving its beautiful coastline. A group of dedicated volunteers, led by local environmental activist Jane Doe, initiated the Hastings Coastal Clean-Up Initiative last month. Since then, they have been working tirelessly to collect litter and debris from beaches, ensuring that this beloved natural resource remains clean and accessible for future generations.

This weekend saw an impressive turnout at the latest coastal clean-up event, where over 100 volunteers turned out despite inclement weather conditions. Local schools and businesses also contributed by providing essential supplies such as gloves and rubbish bags to support the cause.
