A local woman in her eighties is currently receiving medical attention at the hospital following a car accident that occurred earlier this week. The incident took place in Rustington, West Sussex, when the elderly driver's vehicle collided with a nursery facility.

The Hastings & Rye community is deeply concerned about such events and the potential risks they pose to children and vulnerable individuals. Police are actively investigating the crash and have appealed for witnesses or anyone with information related to the incident to come forward.

As the community grapples with this unfortunate event, local authorities urge all drivers to exercise caution and stay alert, especially in areas where young children and elderly people may be present.
