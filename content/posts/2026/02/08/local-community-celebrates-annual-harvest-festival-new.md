The annual Hastings and Rye Harvest Festival was held this weekend in the town centre, drawing crowds from across the region. The event featured a variety of local produce stalls, live music performances, and children's activities. Community groups showcased their work, highlighting the importance of local agriculture and community support.

Organisers reported high visitor numbers, with attendees enjoying the sunny weather and festive atmosphere. This year’s festival also included a special segment dedicated to honouring local farmers and volunteers who contribute significantly to the area’s food supply and community spirit.

The event has become an important part of Hastings and Rye's cultural calendar, promoting local businesses and fostering a sense of community among residents.
