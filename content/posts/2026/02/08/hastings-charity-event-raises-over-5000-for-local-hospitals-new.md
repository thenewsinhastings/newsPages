HASTINGS - A community charity event held in the heart of Hastings has been a resounding success, raising over £5000 for local hospitals. The fundraiser, organised by volunteers from the Hastings Community Health Association, attracted hundreds of attendees who came together to support the cause.

The event featured live music performances, a bake sale, and a silent auction with items donated by local businesses. All proceeds will be used to purchase essential medical equipment for Queen Elizabeth Hospital and St. Leonard's Hospital in Hastings.

Local residents praised the efforts of the organisers and participants alike, highlighting the importance of community support in enhancing healthcare services within their area. The success of this event is set to inspire future initiatives aimed at improving local health facilities.
