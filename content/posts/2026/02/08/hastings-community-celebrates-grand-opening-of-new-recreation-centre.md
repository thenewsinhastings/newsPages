The people of Hastings turned out in droves to celebrate the grand opening of their new state-of-the-art recreation centre on Saturday. Located in the heart of town, the centre offers a wide range of facilities including a swimming pool, sports courts, and community rooms for events.

Local councillors were present at the event, praising the project as a significant investment in the area's infrastructure and quality of life. The new facility promises to offer residents improved opportunities for fitness and leisure activities, fostering stronger social connections within the community.

Organisers have planned a full schedule of activities over the coming weeks to encourage visitors to explore what the recreation centre has to offer. With something for everyone, from family swim sessions to adult fitness classes, there's no doubt that this new addition will become a popular destination in Hastings.
