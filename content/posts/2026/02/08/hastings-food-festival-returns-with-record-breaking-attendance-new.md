The annual Hastings Food Festival returned this weekend with a record-breaking attendance of over 10,000 visitors. Held in the picturesque grounds of Alexandra Park, the event showcased an array of local and international cuisines, offering something for everyone from food enthusiasts to families looking for fun activities.

Local vendors were thrilled with the turnout, reporting increased sales and positive feedback on their offerings. Entertainment included live music stages, cooking demonstrations by renowned chefs, and children's zones filled with games and workshops focused on healthy eating habits.

Organisers noted that this year’s festival also featured a strong focus on sustainability, with initiatives aimed at reducing waste and promoting eco-friendly practices in the food industry. As the event drew to a close, there was widespread enthusiasm for next year’s edition and an eagerness among both vendors and visitors to see what innovations will be introduced.
