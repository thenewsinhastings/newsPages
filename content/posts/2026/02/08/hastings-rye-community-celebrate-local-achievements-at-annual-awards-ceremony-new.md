The annual Hastings and Rye Community Awards ceremony was held last night in the town hall, celebrating outstanding achievements by local residents. Over 200 people attended the event to honour individuals who have made significant contributions to their communities.

Award categories included Volunteer of the Year, Youth Achiever of the Year, and Unsung Hero. The evening was a testament to the strong sense of community spirit in Hastings and Rye, with winners coming from various backgrounds and age groups.
