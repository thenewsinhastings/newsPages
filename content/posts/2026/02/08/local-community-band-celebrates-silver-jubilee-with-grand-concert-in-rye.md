The local community band from Rye celebrated its silver jubilee this weekend with a grand concert at the historic Rye Playhouse. Over two hundred people attended the event, which featured performances ranging from classical to jazz, showcasing the versatility and dedication of the band's members over the past twenty-five years.

Formed in 1998, the Rye Community Band has been an integral part of the cultural fabric of both Rye and Hastings. The silver jubilee concert not only highlighted their musical achievements but also served as a celebration of community spirit and support from local residents who have watched the band grow and evolve over the years.

Band leader John Evans expressed his gratitude to all those involved, saying, 'This milestone is a testament to our members' commitment and the incredible support we've received from the Rye and Hastings communities. We look forward to continuing our musical journey for another twenty-five years.'
