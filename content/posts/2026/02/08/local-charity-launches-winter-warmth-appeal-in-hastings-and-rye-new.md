The local charity, Hands Up for Help, has launched its annual Winter Warmth appeal to support vulnerable residents across Hastings and Rye during the colder months. The initiative aims to provide essential winter supplies such as warm clothing, blankets, and food parcels to those in need.

For more information on how to get involved, visit the Hands Up for Help website.
