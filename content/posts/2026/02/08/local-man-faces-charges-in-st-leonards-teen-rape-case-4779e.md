A man has been charged with the rape of a teenager following an incident reported to have occurred in the St Leonards area, local police confirmed today. The victim, a resident of Hastings & Rye who was identified as being under 18 years old at the time of the alleged assault, is receiving support from local authorities and community services.

Police are urging anyone with information about this case to come forward, as their investigation continues to gather evidence and identify potential witnesses. The accused has been remanded in custody ahead of a court appearance scheduled for next week.

This development highlights the ongoing efforts by law enforcement agencies to address serious crimes within our community and protect vulnerable individuals. Local organizations are also offering support and counseling services to victims and their families.
