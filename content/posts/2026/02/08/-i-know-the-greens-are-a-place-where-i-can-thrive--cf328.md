  "body": "In a significant shift within the local political landscape of Hastings and Rye, Linda Lusingu has officially joined the Green Party after previously serving as an independent councillor following her departure from Labour in 2022. Linda Lusingu expressed her enthusiasm for joining the party, stating, “I know the Greens are a place where I can thrive and contribute meaningfully to our community’s future.” Her transition highlights a growing trend among local politicians in Hastings and Rye who are seeking platforms that align more closely with environmental stewardship and social justice.

This move is expected to influence upcoming discussions on sustainability initiatives, council budget allocations, and community engagement strategies. Residents of Hastings and Rye will be keenly watching how this change impacts local politics and policy-making."
