The local charity organisation 'Hands of Hope' has launched a new appeal to support vulnerable residents in Hastings and Rye during the upcoming winter months. The initiative, named Winter Warmth Appeal, aims to provide essential heating assistance and clothing to those facing financial difficulties.

The charity invites members of the public to donate money, clothing, and essential household items such as blankets and heaters. Drop-off points have been set up at several locations across both towns.
