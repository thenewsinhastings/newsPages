A unique piece of Sussex craftsmanship has hit the headlines as a Brighton-based goldsmith specializes in crafting exceptional trophies for darts players who achieve the remarkable feat of scoring nine consecutive hits. The prestigious 'nine-darter' is now rewarded with a golden dart worth £30,000.

The tradition of celebrating such a rare and skillful accomplishment has been elevated to new heights with these luxurious prizes. Players from Hastings and Rye who aspire to reach this pinnacle in their darts career can look towards the goldsmiths in Brighton as potential custodians of their future accolades, should they achieve this feat.

This local initiative not only highlights the talent within our community but also showcases the craftsmanship and creativity that Sussex is known for. Whether you're a seasoned player or a casual enthusiast, there's something to admire about these golden darts, which now symbolize excellence in the sport across Hastings and Rye.
