Local Olympic athlete Kristen Spours of Team Great Britain has expressed relief and satisfaction after completing her debut training session ahead of the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Games in Milan, Italy. The Hastings-born skater took to the ice on Monday for a rigorous practice run, demonstrating her readiness as she prepares to represent the UK in the upcoming events.

Spours, who has been a significant figure in Hastings’ sports community, stated that after months of intense preparation and competition pressures, this moment allows her to finally relax and enjoy the experience. The local athlete’s participation marks another milestone for the region, with Hastings being known as a hub for producing talented winter sports athletes.

Residents of Hastings and Rye can follow Spours’ progress and support her through the Winter Games, which promise to be an exciting event not just nationally but also locally. With Spours in contention, local sports enthusiasts are looking forward to cheering on their hometown hero.
