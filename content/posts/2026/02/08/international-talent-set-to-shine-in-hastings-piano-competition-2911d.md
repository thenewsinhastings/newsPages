The upcoming Hastings International Piano Competition is set to draw attention from around the globe as 39 young pianists vie for prestigious awards. This annual event, one of the highlights in the town's classical music calendar, will take place at the end of this month and promises to showcase some of the world’s leading young talent.

Organisers are expecting a vibrant display of skill and creativity from participants as they compete for recognition in what has become an important fixture on Hastings’ cultural scene. The competition not only celebrates musical excellence but also highlights the town's commitment to nurturing artistic expression and providing platforms for emerging artists.
