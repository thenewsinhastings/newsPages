Local residents and visitors alike enjoyed the sunny weather as they explored the various attractions on offer. The event was not only an opportunity to celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Hastings and Rye but also a platform for local businesses to promote their products and services.

Organisers reported a significant turnout, with many attendees praising the festival's ability to bring the community together in celebration of all that makes Hastings & Rye special.
