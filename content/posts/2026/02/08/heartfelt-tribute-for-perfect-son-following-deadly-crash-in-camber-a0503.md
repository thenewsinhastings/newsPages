The local community mourns the loss of a young life following a tragic crash that occurred on Wednesday morning in Camber. Police have launched an appeal for witnesses or anyone with information about the incident to come forward, as they continue their investigation into the circumstances surrounding this heartbreaking event.

A heartfelt tribute paid by heartbroken parents has touched many residents in Hastings and Rye, highlighting the positive impact of their son's life within the community. The local area is rallying around those affected, offering support through various means including online memorials and community events planned to honor the deceased's memory.

The police are urging anyone who may have seen or heard anything unusual on Wednesday morning in Camber to contact them immediately as they work tirelessly towards uncovering the truth behind this devastating loss.
