As the Hastings council election season heats up, local candidates are ramping up their campaigns to win votes and make their mark in the upcoming polls. The race is particularly tight this year with several new faces joining established figures vying for positions across various wards.

Community members are encouraged to attend campaign events hosted by these hopefuls, which promise a mix of policy discussions and community engagement activities designed to connect voters directly with those seeking public office. From door-to-door canvassing to town hall meetings, the candidates are making it clear that they are committed to listening and acting on the needs of Hastings residents.

Key issues include local infrastructure improvements, environmental sustainability initiatives, and measures to support small businesses within the community. With election day drawing near, the energy in Hastings is palpable as citizens prepare to cast their votes for the leaders who will shape the future of the town over the next term.
