The United Nations Association (UNA) branch in Hastings is set to host a debate focusing on the pressing issues that undermine world peace. This community event, scheduled for next Thursday at the Town Hall, aims to bring together local residents and experts to discuss current global challenges.

Topics on the agenda include conflicts around the world, human rights violations, climate change, and economic instability. Attendees will have the opportunity to engage in meaningful discussions and explore potential solutions to these complex issues.

This event is open to all members of the community and promises to be an insightful exploration of ways to promote peace and cooperation on a global scale.
