The local branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to convene an important meeting where members will discuss pressing issues undermining world peace. The event, scheduled for next month at the Hastings Town Hall, aims to raise awareness and promote dialogue on global challenges such as conflict resolution, human rights violations, and climate change impacts.

Attendees are expected to include community leaders, local politicians, activists, and concerned citizens from both Hastings and Rye. The UNA branch in Hastings has been actively involved in promoting international understanding and cooperation through various events and initiatives over the past year.

For more information or to get involved, interested individuals can contact the UNA Hastings branch directly at info@una-hastings.org.
