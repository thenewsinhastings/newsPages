The Hastings branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to host an engaging debate focusing on the pressing issues that threaten world peace. The event, scheduled for next month at the local community centre, will feature discussions led by regional experts and community members alike.

Delegates from various backgrounds will present their insights into current global conflicts and challenges, providing a platform for open dialogue and constructive conversation aimed at fostering greater understanding and cooperation among Hastings residents.

All interested parties are invited to participate in what promises to be an enlightening evening. The debate aims not only to raise awareness of these critical issues but also to inspire local action towards promoting peace on both national and international levels.
