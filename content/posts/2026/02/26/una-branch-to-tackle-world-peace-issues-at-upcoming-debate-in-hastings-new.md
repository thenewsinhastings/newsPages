The United Nations Association (UNA) branch based in Hastings will host a debate focusing on the pressing issues that undermine world peace. Set to take place next week, the event aims to raise awareness and encourage discussion among local residents about the challenges facing global stability.

Key topics up for discussion include geopolitical tensions, environmental crises, and human rights violations around the globe. Local experts and community members are invited to share their perspectives on these critical issues and contribute to finding solutions for a more peaceful world.

The UNA branch in Hastings is dedicated to promoting international understanding and cooperation through various educational initiatives and events like this debate. Attendees will have an opportunity to engage with speakers, ask questions, and participate in meaningful dialogue.
