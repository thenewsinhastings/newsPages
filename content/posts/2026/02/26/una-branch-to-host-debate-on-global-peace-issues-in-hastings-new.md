The UN Association (UNA) branch in Hastings is set to host a debate focusing on the pressing issues that are undermining global peace. The event, which will take place at the Hastings Town Hall next week, aims to engage local residents and experts from various fields in discussions about conflict resolution, international cooperation, and humanitarian challenges.

The UNA Hastings Branch encourages all members of the community interested in contributing to or learning more about these critical issues to attend. With a range of speakers scheduled to participate, the debate promises to be an enlightening experience for those seeking to understand the complexities of global peace initiatives and their local implications.
