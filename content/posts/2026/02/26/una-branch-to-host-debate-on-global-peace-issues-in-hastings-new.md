The local branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to host an important debate addressing pressing issues that threaten global peace. The event, scheduled for next week at the Town Hall in Hastings, will bring together community members and experts to discuss topics such as climate change, economic disparities, and international conflicts.

Speakers from various backgrounds will share insights on how these challenges impact communities locally and globally, aiming to inspire action towards promoting a more peaceful world. Members of the public are invited to attend this informative event, which aims to foster dialogue and understanding among attendees.

For those interested in participating or learning more about the UNA's mission to promote international cooperation, visit their website or contact the local branch for further information.
