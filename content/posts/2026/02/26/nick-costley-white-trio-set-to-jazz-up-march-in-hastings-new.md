Local jazz enthusiasts have something to look forward to this month as the Nick Costley-White Trio prepares for its eagerly anticipated gig at Jazz Hastings. The trio, known for their lively and engaging performances, will bring a fresh twist to classic jazz tunes, appealing to both seasoned fans and newcomers alike.

The event promises an evening filled with musical excellence, showcasing the talents of Nick Costley-White alongside his bandmates. Known for their innovative approach to traditional jazz styles, the trio's performance is expected to draw crowds from across Hastings and beyond.

Jazz Hastings continues its mission to promote local talent while also welcoming renowned acts like the Nick Costley-White Trio. This initiative not only enhances the cultural landscape of the area but also fosters a vibrant community spirit among music lovers.
