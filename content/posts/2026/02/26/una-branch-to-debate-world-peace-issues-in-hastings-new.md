The local branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to convene a significant debate this week, focusing on the pressing issues that continue to undermine world peace. The event will take place in Hastings and aims to provide a platform for informed discussion among community members, academics, and activists who are concerned about global stability.

Scheduled for next Tuesday at 6:30 PM at the Town Hall, attendees will have the opportunity to engage with experts from various fields who will share insights on topics such as conflict resolution, international cooperation, and the role of technology in promoting peace. The event is open to all members of the public and promises to be a vital contribution to ongoing dialogue about global security and harmony.

For more information or to register for the debate, visit the UNA Hastings chapter's website.
