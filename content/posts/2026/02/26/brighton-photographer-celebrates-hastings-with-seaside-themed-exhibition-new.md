A Brighton-based photographer has expressed his deep connection to Hastings through a series of photographs that capture the essence and charm of the seaside town. JJ Waller, known for his evocative images of coastal scenes, says photographing Hastings is both an 'affinity' and a source of 'warmth'. His exhibition, which opens next week at the local gallery, promises to showcase the unique beauty and character of Hastings through a photographer's lens.
