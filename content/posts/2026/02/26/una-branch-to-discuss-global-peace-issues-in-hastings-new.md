The local branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) is set to host a debate on Wednesday evening focusing on the pressing issues that are currently undermining world peace. The event, taking place at the Hastings Town Hall, will feature discussions on conflict resolution, international cooperation, and strategies for promoting global stability.

Speakers from various backgrounds, including academics, activists, and community leaders, will share their insights and experiences to encourage a wider conversation about peacebuilding initiatives within our region and beyond. The public is invited to attend this free event and contribute to the dialogue by asking questions and sharing their perspectives on how we can collectively work towards a more peaceful world.

For those interested in participating or learning more, please contact the local UNA branch via email at hasting.sunab@gmail.com for further details.
