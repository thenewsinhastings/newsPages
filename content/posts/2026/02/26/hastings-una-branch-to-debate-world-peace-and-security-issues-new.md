The Hastings branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) will host a debate on pressing issues undermining world peace, providing an opportunity for community members to engage in discussions about global security concerns.

Scheduled to take place at the local library this coming Saturday, the event aims to bring together individuals from diverse backgrounds and perspectives. Participants will explore various topics affecting international stability such as conflict resolution, arms control, and humanitarian crises. The debate is open to all residents interested in contributing to or learning about efforts towards a more peaceful world.

The Hastings UNA branch encourages attendance for those who wish to be part of constructive conversations that could lead to positive changes in global politics.
