As the election season heats up in Hastings and Rye, local candidates are stepping up their campaigns to win seats on the council. With numerous community issues at stake, including housing, transportation, and environmental concerns, residents are keeping a close eye on the developments.

The upcoming elections will see several new faces joining the existing councillors as they aim to bring fresh perspectives and innovative solutions to the table. Candidates have been engaging with the public through various forums, meetings, and social media platforms to share their policies and vision for the future of Hastings and Rye.

For more updates on the election campaigns and information about your local candidates, keep an eye out for further coverage in our upcoming issues.
