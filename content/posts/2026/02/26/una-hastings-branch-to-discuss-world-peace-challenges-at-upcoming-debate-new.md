Attendees are invited to engage in an open dialogue about topics such as international relations, human rights violations, and economic disparities, all of which contribute to instability on a worldwide scale. The aim is to raise awareness among local residents about these critical issues while encouraging informed discussions that can lead to positive change both locally and globally.
