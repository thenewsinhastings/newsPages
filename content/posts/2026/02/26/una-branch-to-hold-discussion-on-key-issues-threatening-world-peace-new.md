The local branch of the United Nations Association (UNA) will convene a meeting next week to discuss pressing issues that threaten global peace and stability. The event, open to members and interested community members, aims to explore topics such as conflict resolution, human rights advocacy, and international cooperation.

Scheduled for Tuesday at Hastings Town Hall, the session is expected to attract professionals, activists, and concerned citizens who are passionate about promoting peaceful relations between nations. Attendees will have the opportunity to contribute their perspectives on how local communities can play a role in addressing global challenges.

For those interested in attending or learning more about the UNA's initiatives in Hastings, contact details for joining the discussion group can be found at www.unahastings.org.
