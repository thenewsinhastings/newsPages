The annual Hastings International Piano Concerto Competition is set to attract young pianists from around the world with this year's event boasting a record-breaking number of participants. Thirty-nine talented musicians have signed up for the prestigious competition, each vying for recognition and the chance to perform alongside renowned orchestras.

Taking place in the heart of Hastings, the competition offers a unique opportunity for these young performers to showcase their skills on an international stage while also contributing to the vibrant cultural life of the town. This event has become a significant highlight in the local calendar, drawing audiences from across Sussex and beyond.

Local businesses and residents are encouraged to support the upcoming festival by attending performances and engaging with the participating artists. The competition promises not only exceptional musical talent but also an enriching experience for all attendees.
