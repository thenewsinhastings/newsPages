Police in Hastings are appealing for public assistance as they investigate the death of a woman discovered at a local construction site. The victim, described by police as a beloved mother, grandmother, and sister, met with her tragic end under circumstances that officials say were avoidable.

Detectives have issued an urgent request to any individuals who may have seen or captured on camera a specific vehicle around the time of the incident. They hope these sightings could provide crucial information leading to the identification and apprehension of those involved.

Community members are encouraged to come forward with any relevant information that might aid in this ongoing investigation, as police work diligently to ensure justice for the deceased and peace for her loved ones.
