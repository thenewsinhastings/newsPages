The annual Hastings Fat Tuesday event is seeking the community's support through the sale of raffle tickets. Proceeds from these ticket sales will go towards funding next year’s celebration, ensuring that this beloved local tradition can continue to bring joy and excitement to residents and visitors alike.

Organisers are inviting everyone to contribute by purchasing a raffle ticket. Not only does buying a ticket help secure the future of Hastings Fat Tuesday, but it also offers the chance to win some fantastic prizes, making it an exciting way to support your community.

For those interested in participating or learning more about how they can get involved, details and tickets are available from local businesses and online through the official Hastings Fat Tuesday website. This year’s event was a huge success, showcasing the vibrancy and spirit of Hastings and its surrounding areas.
