The Hastings Museum has unveiled a new exhibition showcasing the work of local artist JJ Waller. The exhibition features a diverse collection of paintings and drawings, highlighting Waller’s unique artistic style and his deep connection to the Hastings community.

Waller, known for his vibrant and intricate pieces that capture the essence of everyday life in Hastings, said he was delighted to have this opportunity to share his work with the public. The exhibition is a celebration not only of his art but also of the rich cultural tapestry that defines Hastings.

Visitors are invited to explore the exhibition from now until mid-December. Admission is free, making it accessible for all members of the community to enjoy and appreciate Waller’s talent. For those interested in attending, the museum offers extended hours during weekends to accommodate various schedules.
