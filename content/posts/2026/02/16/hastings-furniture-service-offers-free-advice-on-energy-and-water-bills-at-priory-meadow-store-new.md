Residents of Hastings can now seek free expert advice on managing their energy and water bills at the Hastings Furniture Service's store in Priory Meadow. The initiative aims to provide much-needed support to local households facing financial difficulties due to rising utility costs.

The service, which is available every Thursday from 10 AM to 2 PM, offers practical tips and solutions for reducing monthly outgoings while ensuring homes remain warm and well-maintained throughout the year. Interested individuals are encouraged to visit the store during these hours or call ahead to book an appointment.

Store Manager at Hastings Furniture Service said: 'We are proud to offer this community service, helping our neighbours navigate the challenges of increasing utility prices. Our dedicated team is here to provide guidance and resources that can make a real difference in people's lives.'

For more information or to schedule an appointment, please contact the Priory Meadow store directly.
