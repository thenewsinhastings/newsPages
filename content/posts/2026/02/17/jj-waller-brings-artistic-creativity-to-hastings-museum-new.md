Local artist JJ Waller has taken up residence at the Hastings Museum this week, bringing a fresh wave of creative energy and artistic inspiration to the community. Waller is known for his vibrant paintings that capture the essence of local life and landscapes.

During his time at the museum, visitors will have the opportunity to witness firsthand as Waller creates new works of art inspired by the rich history and heritage found within the Hastings Museum's collections. This interactive experience aims to foster a deeper connection between the community and their cultural heritage through visual storytelling.

The event is part of the ongoing efforts to enhance cultural engagement in Hastings, showcasing the importance of local talent and creativity as a vital component of community life.
