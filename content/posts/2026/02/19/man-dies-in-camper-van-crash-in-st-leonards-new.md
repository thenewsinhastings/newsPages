A tragic incident occurred on Monday evening when a man died at the scene of a crash involving a camper van in St Leonards. Police are appealing for any witnesses or individuals with information to come forward.

The accident took place in a busy area, highlighting concerns over road safety within the community. Authorities have asked locals to remain vigilant and report any suspicious activity that could provide clues about the circumstances leading up to the crash.
